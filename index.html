About Me

Let's Start off with a little introduction

Hi, my name is Jacob and I am a Game Developer. My whole life I've enjoyed playing video games from mobile games, to pc and console games, and ever since the covid lockdown started I've developed a passion for game development. fast Forward today I am creating this project to publish my christian games.

Why Christian Games?

Here's why

I want to try and use my tallent to glorify God, my goal is to create educational as well as fun, and engaging christian games, I also want to use this project as an opportunity to get closer with God, I'm thinking of adding a page in this website called "My Journey" where everyone could share their journey in Christianity.
